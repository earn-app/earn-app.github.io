How it works

Choose a promotion

A local business will post a new offer or promotion, It could be house painting, plumbing, restaurant, retail, coffee shop or anything else. Choose the offers you want to promote based on the business or payout.

Share the offer

Use a unique link that just for you and share with your networks, anytime a sale or a lead comes through that link, you get credited for it.

Track your earnings

On your dashboard, you can promote offers, see new offers, and track your progress and earnings.

Cash out

You can let your account balance accumulate and earn interest, or you can cash out via check by mail, paypal, or zelle.
